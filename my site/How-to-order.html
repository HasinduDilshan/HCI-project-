HOW TO ORDER

Step 01

Go to our online shop tab

Step 02

When you have done all your shopping, go to check out (top right hand button)

Step 03

Select your delivery area or postcode ( if you can not see your area please email us and we will see if we can deliver to your area)

Step 04

Your delivery date will automatically be updated

Step 05

Update your name, address and phone number details

Step 06

Fill in any additional notes in the box if you have any special instructions

Step 07

Press the red tab at the bottom of the page to checkout and this will take you to the payment gateway.

Step 08

Make your payment.
You will receive a email confirmation

Step 09

We will contact you on the number you provided us the day before your delivery with a 2 hour delivery slot.Give us a try and HAPPY shopping.
