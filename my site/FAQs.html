FAQs

CAN I FREEZE THE MEAT ONCE IT HAS ARRIVED?

Yes all the meats can be placed in a freezer. The packaging will state if it is fresh or frozen.The frozen meats have been frozen solid and put straight into our purpose built refrigerated vehicles straight to your door.The fresh products have never been frozen so either one is suitable for home freezing.
